Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam



Early Life:

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, into a modest family. His early life was marked by hardship, yet he pursued his education with determination.


Education:

He studied physics and aerospace engineering, graduating from Madras Institute of Technology (MIT).


Career:

Dr. Kalam contributed significantly to India's defense and space programs as a scientist at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).


Philosophy:

Known for his simplicity and belief in the power of dreams, Dr. Kalam famously said, "Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts, and thoughts result in action."
